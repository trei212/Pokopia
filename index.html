Habitat & Pokédex Reference
  • Pokémon view — filter by specialty, name, zone, time, weather, or preferences (environment, flavor, likes). Tap any pill on a card to filter by that attribute.
  • Habitat view — search by habitat name, Pokémon name, zone, rarity, or items you have (comma-separated). Tap a Pokémon sprite to jump to their card.
  • Navigation — tap a habitat chip on a Pokémon card to jump to that habitat. Use ← Back to return.
  • Specialty pills — tap any specialty to see all Pokémon with that skill.
